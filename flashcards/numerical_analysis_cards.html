What is \(\text{ Theorem оп Derivative and Divided Difference}\)
If \( f \) is \( n \) times continuously differentiable on \([a, b]\) and if \(x_0, x_1, \ldots, x_n\) are distinct points in \([a, b]\), then there exists a point \( \xi \) in \((a, b)\) such that \[ f[x_0, x_1, \ldots, x_n] = \frac{1}{n!} f^{(n)}(\xi) \]
