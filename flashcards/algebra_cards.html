What is the definition of a \(\text{ Simple Group}\)?
A simple group is a nontrivial group whose only normal subgroups are the trivial group and the group itself (i.e. \{e\} and \(G\)).
What is \(\text{ Mean Value Theorem}\)?
\[ \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}=f'(c),\text{ where }c\in(a,b) \]
What is the condition number of \(f(x)\)?
\[ \kappa=\frac{|xf'(x)|}{|f(x)|} \]
