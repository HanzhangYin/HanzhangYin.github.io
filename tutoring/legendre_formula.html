Legendre's Formula

Formula

For any prime number \( p \) and any positive integer \( n\in\mathbb{Z}^+ \), let \( \nu_p(n) \) be the exponent of the largest power of \( p \) that divides \( n \) (that is, the \( p \)-adic valuation of \( n \)). Then \[ \nu_p(n!) = \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \left\lfloor \frac{n}{p^i} \right\rfloor , \] where \( \lfloor x \rfloor \) is the floor function.
