Foulkes Conjecture

\(\textbf{Conjecture.}\) Let \(a, b \in \IN_{>0}\) with \(a \leq b\). Then for every partition \(\lambda\) the multiplicity of the irreducible \(\GL(V)\) representation \(\{\lambda\}\) in the plethysm \(\Sym^a \Sym^b V\) is at most as large as the multiplicity of \(\{\lambda\}\) in \(\Sym^b \Sym^a V\).
