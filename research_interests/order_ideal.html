Order Ideal

\(\textbf{Definition: }\)A set \(A\) is an antichain (or Sperner family or clutter) of a poset \(P\) if \(A\subset P\) and any pair of elements of \(A\) is incomparable in \(P\). The collection of all antichains of \(P\) is denoted \(\mathrm{Anti(P)}\), and the poset induced by inclusion on \(\mathrm{Anti(P)}\) is denoted ambiguously by \(\mathrm{Anti(P)}\).
